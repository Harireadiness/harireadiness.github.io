Most AI programmes stall not because of the technology — but because of the human system beneath it. HARI is the diagnostic instrument built to measure that system and identify exactly where it is constraining value.

HARI produces a diagnostic finding — not a survey score. It identifies the specific organisational conditions limiting AI value in your business, and sequences what needs to change first.

The instrument is currently in its founding cohort phase. Full details and access will be published shortly.
